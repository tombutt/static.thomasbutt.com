What I do

I don't like to be classified simply by my job title and specifications. What I do is much broader. I investigate and leverage new technologies, within my job or on my own. I've developed iOS apps personally and professionally, I've used AWS technology for myself and my company, and I strive to use the best solutions available to solve the problems encountered in a rapidly changing technological landscape.

Some people use technology for the sake of technology. I find that due to many factors (cost, security, simplicity, maintainability, and others), sometimes the latest technology isn't the best choice. For example, this is a static website, not an ASP site, or CMS. I've done both, but find that for the cost, maintenance, and overall performance, a static site with some CSS is the best solution.

Why a Resume Site

The days of a simple paper resume are coming to an end in the technology world. This is an attempt to provide you with more information than can be contained on any single piece of paper. I've seen great resumes, and I've seen horrible ones. Often they don't reflect the individual, hopefully this will present you with a fuller picture of my desires, abilities, and direction.
